Dr. Julian Bernauer

I'm a Postdoc Researcher at the Data and Methods Unit of the MZES in Mannheim, Germany.

Here you'll find information on my research in political science and methodology.

I'm interested in political institutions, representation and quantitative methods. Currently, my focus is on the quantitative analysis of political texts and the measurement and politics of populism.
